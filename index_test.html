Walmart Sales Forecasting

Python

A systematic comparison of hierarchical forecasting strategies (granular vs. aggregate-disaggregate) using Holt-Winters on Walmart retail data.

3.96% better RMSE 15% less noise

The Challenge: Should we forecast at granular department level, or aggregate first and disaggregate later? Granularity can amplify noise in volatile retail data.

My Approach: Compared both strategies with Holt-Winters smoothing, using ADF/KPSS/Mann-Kendall tests for model selection and grid search for parameter tuning.

Impact: The aggregate-disaggregate method beat granular forecasting by 3.96% RMSE while cutting noise 15%, actionable insight for retail supply-chain optimization.
